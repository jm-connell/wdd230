About Us

Where We Started

Bountiful Foods all started with our founder Scott Matthews' love for gardening. He started selling selling produce from his home garden in 2002. Eventually he was able to quit his job as an accountant to work with local farmers to bring their fresh product straight to the local population of the Carlsbad area.

Now, Bountiful Foods is home to over 20 employees working with 12 different farmers from all around Southern California. Our passion is delicious, locally grown fruits and veggies. We hope to serve you soon!
