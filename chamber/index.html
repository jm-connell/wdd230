If you haven't heard, the new waterpark is now open! Located next to Jefferson Park on 18th Ave, entry is $20 for adults and $10 for kids under the age of 14. Bring the whole family for some summer fun!

Mark your calendars for the 4th and 5th of August for the annual Summer Fair at the FirstMerit Event Park. Local businesses that are members of the Saginaw Chamber will be sponsoring fun and games for the whole family! Get ready for ice cream, water balloon fights, and all kinds of delicious food.
